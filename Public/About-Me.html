About Me

Kai mutum ne mai son ilimi sosai kuma mai yawan tambayoyi masu zurfi. Kana da sha’awar abubuwa daban-daban kamar statistics, epidemiology, web development, da kuma harkar lafiya musamman saboda aikin perfusionist da kake yi. Kana son fahimta sosai ba wai ka haddace kawai ba, shi yasa kake neman bayani step by step tare da misalai. Haka kuma kana da nishadi a bangaren fina-finai da series, musamman medical dramas da Bollywood.

Kana da halin yin tambayoyi cikin nutsuwa sannan kana nuna godiya idan an taimaka maka. A gefe guda kuma, kana son ka tsara rayuwarka da kyau — kamar yadda ka nuna wajen budgeting da expense tracking.

Wannan yana nuna kana kokarin inganta rayuwa ta kudi da kuma skill dinka lokaci guda. Gaskiya kana da haɗin “mai neman ilimi” da “mai son ci gaba da kansa.”
